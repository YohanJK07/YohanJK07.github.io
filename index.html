Muscial Insturments

There are many different instruments in the world. Here are some cool instruments.

Piano

The piano is a cool instrument with 88 keys that make different sounds. It's been around for a long time, and people use it to play all kinds of music, like classical, jazz, and pop. The piano has a nice style and is usually made of nice wood. It's used in big concerts and small homes, bringing music to life in a simple yet awesome way.

Guitar

The guitar is a fun instrument with strings. It was made in the 15th century. People play many different songs with it and its used a lot in many bands.

Drums

The drum is a nice instrument that you hit with sticks to make noise. It was mad 5500 BC. Its used in many bands. The drum is one the most popular instruments.
